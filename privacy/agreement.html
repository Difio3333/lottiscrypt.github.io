Privacy Policy for LottisCrypt

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the LottisCrypt mobile application (the "App") for iOS and Android. LottisCrypt was built with a strict offline-first, zero-trust architecture. We believe your privacy is a fundamental right.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We collect exactly zero personal data. LottisCrypt does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your data. We do not use analytics software, we do not track your usage, and we do not collect crash reports.

3. How Your Data is Processed

All cryptographic operations—including key generation, file encryption, and file decryption—occur strictly locally on your physical device.

  • Your Keys: Your cryptographic keys (Identity Keys and Signing Keys) are generated and stored exclusively in your device's secure local storage (such as the Apple Secure Enclave / Keychain or Android Keystore). They never leave your device.
  • Your Files: The files you choose to encrypt or decrypt are processed locally. We do not have access to your files, your keys, or the contents of your encrypted data.

4. Third-Party Services

LottisCrypt does not integrate with any third-party tracking, advertising, or data-harvesting services. There are no external servers involved in the core encryption or decryption processes.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page. However, our core philosophy will never change: we do not want your data.
